Our Sustainable Materials

The science behind our eco-friendly packaging

PLA (Polylactic Acid)

A biodegradable thermoplastic made from renewable resources like corn starch or sugarcane. PLA breaks down into water and carbon dioxide in commercial composting facilities.

Mushroom Packaging

Created from agricultural waste and mushroom mycelium, this packaging is 100% home compostable and biodegrades within 45 days of disposal.

Seaweed-Based Films

Transparent films made from seaweed extracts that dissolve in water and are edible, offering zero waste packaging for appropriate products.
